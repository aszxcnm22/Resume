Published Research

MRI-Based Bladder Cancer Staging via YOLOv11 Segmentation and Deep Learning Classification

Automated MRI-based deep learning framework for improving bladder cancer staging consistency, especially for distinguishing non-muscle-invasive disease from muscle-invasive cancer.

  • Combined YOLOv11 for tumor detection, DeepLabV3 for segmentation refinement, and VGG19, ResNet50, and Vision Transformer for stage prediction.
  • Evaluated 416 T2-weighted MRI images labeled T1 to T4 with classification metrics and bootstrap confidence intervals.
  • Results showed strong performance for separating non-muscle-invasive from muscle-invasive cases, supporting more standardized MRI-based staging research.
